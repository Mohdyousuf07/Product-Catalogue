Ford Mustang


Price : 74.5 lakh /-₹

Detalis :

The 1967 Ford Mustang is an iconic American muscle car known for its classic design and powerful performance. As part of the original Mustang generation, it features a long hood, short rear deck, and a sleek profile that captures the essence of 1960s automotive styling. Under the hood, it offered a range of engine options, including the base 200-cubic-inch inline-six and powerful V8 variants, with the most notable being the 289 cubic inch (4.7-liter) engine producing up to 271 horsepower. The 1967 Mustang introduced several design changes, including a wider body and larger windows, enhancing both aesthetics and driver visibility. Inside, it boasts a driver-oriented cockpit with options for luxurious features like vinyl upholstery and a rally pack gauge cluster. With its combination of style, performance, and customization options, the 1967 Mustang remains a beloved classic among car enthusiasts and collectors.
